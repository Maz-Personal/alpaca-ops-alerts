Automated operational & risk alerts for a personal algorithmic trading system.

TradeOps Alerts is a private monitoring application, operated by an individual, that watches an automated (paper) trading system and its brokerage connection and texts the account owner when something needs attention — a brokerage gateway going offline, a recovery, or a daily-loss safety limit being hit.

What this service is

TradeOps Alerts is not a consumer product and not a marketing service. It is a personal, self-hosted monitoring system run by its owner. The software watches the owner's own automated trading services and the brokerage API connections they depend on, and sends the owner an account notification by SMS when a monitored condition changes.

  • Sender: TradeOps Alerts — an automated software system (no human composes the messages).
  • Recipient: the account owner only — the single individual who runs the system and enrolled their own mobile number.
  • Purpose: operational health and trading-risk safety notifications.

How it works & what you'll receive

The owner enrolls by submitting their own mobile number (below) and enabling alerts in the application's private configuration. From then on, the system sends automated notifications only when a monitored event occurs. Typical volume is low — up to roughly 10 messages per day, and often zero on a healthy day.

Example messages

  • TradeOps Alerts: Brokerage gateway is down (logged out / unreachable). Automated system alert. Reply STOP to opt out.
  • TradeOps Alerts: Brokerage gateway recovered — back online. Automated notification.
  • TradeOps Alerts: Daily loss cap reached — automated trading halted. Risk alert.
  • TradeOps Alerts: Brokerage gateway is down. Reply YES to authorize an automatic restart, or ignore to leave it. Msg & data rates may apply; reply STOP to opt out.

Some notifications ask the owner to reply YES to authorize one specific, pre-defined action (for example, restarting a service). This is a transactional confirmation, not a conversation — the system does not chat.
