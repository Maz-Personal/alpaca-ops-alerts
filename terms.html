TradeOps Alerts ("the Service") is a private, self-hosted monitoring application operated by an individual. It sends automated operational and risk account-notification text messages to the account owner about the status of the owner's own automated trading services and brokerage API connections. The Service is not a consumer product, is not offered to the public, and sends no marketing messages.

3. Opt-out and help

You may cancel the SMS service at any time by replying STOP to any message. After you send STOP, we will send one confirmation message and then send no further messages unless you re-enroll. For help, reply HELP or email maz.zabaneh@gmail.com.

Certain notifications may invite you to reply YES to authorize a single, pre-defined operational action (for example, restarting a service). A reply is treated only as authorization for that one specific action and for no other purpose. The Service does not conduct conversational messaging.

6. Acceptable use

The Service is intended solely for the owner's own account and number. You will not use it to send messages to any person who has not personally enrolled their own number.
