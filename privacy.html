Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 1, 2026

TradeOps Alerts ("the Service") is a private, self-hosted monitoring application operated by an individual for their own use. This policy explains how the mobile number used for SMS notifications is handled.

Information collected

The only personal information the Service uses is the mobile phone number the owner enrolls in order to receive automated account-notification text messages, together with the delivery metadata your carrier and the messaging provider generate to send those messages.

How it is used

The number is used for one purpose only: to deliver the operational and risk account-notification messages described on our home page and Terms of Service. It is stored in the owner's own private server configuration and in the messaging provider (Twilio) used to send the messages.

No sharing, no sale, no marketing

We do not sell, rent, or share your mobile number or messaging data with any third party. Phone numbers and SMS consent are never shared with third parties or affiliates for their marketing or promotional purposes. The number is disclosed only to the messaging provider (Twilio) strictly to transmit the messages you enrolled to receive, and as required by law.

Opt-out

You can stop all messages at any time by replying STOP. You may request deletion of your number from the configuration by emailing maz.zabaneh@gmail.com.

Data retention & security

The number is retained only for as long as alerts are enabled and is removed on opt-out or on request. It is held on a private server and with the messaging provider under their respective security controls.

Children

The Service is not directed to children and does not knowingly collect information from anyone under 18.

Changes

This policy may be updated from time to time; the "Last updated" date reflects the current version.

Contact

Questions about this policy or your data: maz.zabaneh@gmail.com.
